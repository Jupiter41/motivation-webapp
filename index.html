Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Welcome to NextLevel. Your privacy matters to us, and we take data protection seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and what rights you have regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Account information: email address, username, or authentication identifiers
  • User-generated content: goals, quests, progress, preferences, and settings
  • Usage data: interactions with the app, feature usage, and performance metrics
  • Technical data: device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as medical records, precise location, contacts, or payment card information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the core features of the application
  • Saving your progress and personalizing your experience
  • Improving stability, performance, and usability
  • Preventing abuse, fraud, and security issues
  • Communicating important service-related information

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, your data is processed under the following legal bases:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract (providing the app’s services)
  • Legitimate interests such as security and improvement of the service

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be shared only in limited cases:

  • With trusted infrastructure providers (e.g. hosting, analytics, crash reporting)
  • When required by law or legal obligation
  • To protect the rights, safety, or integrity of users and the service

All third-party services are required to comply with strict confidentiality and data protection standards.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and secure storage solutions. However, no system can be 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service or until you request deletion. Inactive accounts may be periodically reviewed and removed.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

9. Children’s Privacy

NextLevel is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe such data has been collected, please contact us immediately.
